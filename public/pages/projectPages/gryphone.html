GryPhone
##Description *** GryPhone is designed to provide vital information from the University of Guelph and the city of Guelph to the Android mobile platform, GryPhone provides the latest news & events for student, real-time bus arrival times for the city of Guelph, library search and access to online services such as Courselink, Webadvisor and GryphMail. In addition GryPhone provides an interactive campus map and a handy note taking feature. GryPhone is created by a first-year student Tornike Natsvlishvili from the University of Guelph to make tasks like those mentioned above easier for all students. ##Screenshots ![Gryphone home screen](dist/assets/imgs/gryphone_main.png) ![Gryphone events page](dist/assets/imgs/gryphone_events.png)
